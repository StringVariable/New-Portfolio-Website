Contact us

Need to get in touch with us?

Need to speak to a real person? Don’t worry, we have you covered. We understand that superannuation is a lot of information to take in at once, especially to those young people who have never had experience with it before. That’s why we’re here, we’re here to answer any questions or concerns you may have about the process. Our offices are located at 52 Alice street, Brisbane CBD where you can come on in and say hi in person while we try to find the right super for you. If you can’t make it into our offices, we have a support phone line for any queries available on 3352 2453. If you’d rather just a quick question answered,our email is available 24/7 at info@bestsuper.com. If you have any pressing question right now, feel free to fill out the contact form below!
